About Prasad Realty

Welcome to **Prasad Realty**, your trusted partner in navigating the dynamic real estate landscape of Visakhapatnam. Founded by **Chalamkuri V.S.S Prasad**, our firm is built on principles of transparency, integrity, and a deep understanding of the local market.

With years of experience, we specialize in helping clients find their ideal **Flats/Apartments, Independent Houses, Builder Floors, Commercial Shops, and Residential Land/Plots**. Our extensive reach covers prime locations including Visakhapatnam, Gajuwaka, Kurmannapalem, Duvvada, Lankelapalem, Sabbavaram, Aganampudi, and Pedagantyada.

At Prasad Realty, we are committed to providing personalized service, ensuring every client's real estate journey is seamless and successful. Whether you are looking to buy, sell, or invest, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
